Why Call Your Elected Officials?

Want to have your voice heard and actually influence public policy? Forget social media. The best way to be heard is through a phone call!

But Calling Is Hard...

We get it! First, you have to figure out who represents you, what they stand for, and what you should say. That's when good intentions quickly fade away into inaction.

...So We made it easy!

You're busy. So are we. That's why we're putting together tools that will help make it easier than ever to call your elected representatives.
